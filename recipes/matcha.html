Iced Matcha Latte

Every cafe needs a drink! This matcha latte recipe is cool and refreshing. It's creamy and gives a nice energy boost that is much appreciated when petting lots of dogs at the cafe!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz milk
  • 2 tsp matcha powder
  • 2 tsp vanilla syrup, honey, or sugar
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. To the bowl of a blender, add the milk, matcha powder, and vanilla syrup.Blend for 30 seconds to 1 minute or until the matcha is lump free.
  2. Pour into a cup of ice and Enjoy!
